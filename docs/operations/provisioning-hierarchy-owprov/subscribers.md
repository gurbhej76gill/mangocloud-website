Use cases:

- Create subscriber accounts for end users (User Portal / mobile app).
- Assign devices to a subscriber for ownership and subscriber-level configuration.
- Apply subscriber-specific settings while inheriting Entity/Venue defaults.

Steps:

1. Log in to OWPROV-UI: `https://<DOMAIN_NAME>:8443`.
2. Go to Operators.

![Subscriber navigation](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image31.png)

3. Select the Operator.
4. Open the Subscribers section/tab.
5. Click + (Create).

![Create subscriber](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image59.png)

6. Fill in Subscriber details (email, name, password, email validation).

![Subscriber details form](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image57.png)

7. Click Save.

![Subscribers list](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image5.png)

8. Confirm the subscriber appears in the list.

Result: The subscriber is created and can be used to associate devices and apply subscriber-level provisioning policies.
